Honey Cheese Toast

From annachaannnn on TikTok

This is one of my go-to breakfasts in the morning. Its super easy to make and quick. Perfect for busy mornings!

Ingredients:

  • your choice of bread (I recommend white)
  • Kewpie Mayo
  • garlic powder
  • your choice of cheese
  • chili flakes
  • pepper
  • honey
  • parsley flakes

Steps

  1. Spread Kewpie mayo on your bread and sprinkle garlic powder on top. Then top with your choice of cheese.
  2. Put it in the airfryer at 350° for 5 minutes.
  3. Take it out of the airfryer, then drizzle with honey and add chili flakes, pepper, and parsley flakes.
  4. Finally, enjoy!
