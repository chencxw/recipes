Couscous Salad

From Wellness by Kay

This is one of my absolute favourite recipe. It's couscous salad with avocado and corn. It's easy to make and pairs well with any dish!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup pearl or regular couscous, dry
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup fresh mozzarella pearls
  • 15 oz can corn
  • 1 avocado
  • 1/4 cup balsamic vinaigrette
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • salt and pepper
  • fresh basil

Steps

  1. Cook the couscous according to its instructions on the package.
  2. Drain and rinse the can of corn. Next, add olive oil and the rinsed corn to a pan on the stove over medium to high heat. Add salt and pepper to taste and satué until corn is nicely browned. Tip: Don't mix the corn around too much!
  3. Remove both the couscous and corn from the heat and let them cool completely. (You can place it in the fridge to speed up the process!)
  4. Halve the cherry tomatoes and cut the avocado into cubes.
  5. Into a large mixing bowl, add the couscous, halved cherry tomatoes, cubed avocados, roasted corn, mozzarella pearls, balsamic vinaigrette, and salt & pepper to taste. Mix until combined evenly and garnish with some fresh basil.
  6. Let it cool in the fridge before serving and enjoy!
