Spicy Garlic Noodles

Recipe and picture by Tiffy Cooks

A super easy and delicious meal to make, and it only takes 10 minutes! This recipe is super customizeable as well. Even my mom is obsessed with this recipe!

Ingredients:

  • 2 servings of any noodle of your choice (I highly recommend wide hand-cut noodles)
  • 1.5 tbsp of chili oil
  • 2 tbsp of soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp of black vinegar
  • 1 tbsp of crushed red chili flakes
  • 0.5 tbsp sesame seeds (to garnish)
  • 4 tbsp of oil
  • 5 cloves of minced garlic
  • julienned cucumbers
  • cilantro
  • green onion
  • eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles according to its instructions on the package.
  2. In a bowl add: 2 tbsp soy sauce, 3 tbsp black vinegar, 1.5 tbsp chili oil, crushed red chili flakes, sesame seeds, and minced garlic on top.
  3. Heat 4 tbsp of oil.
  4. Pour the hot oil over the garlic. Let it sizzle then mix.
  5. Drain the noodles and add it to a large bowl. Add the hot oil mixture in and mix.
  6. Top with cucumbers, green onions, cilantro, and fried egg. And Enjoy!
